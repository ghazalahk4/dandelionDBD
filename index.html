Dandelion

Gender-smart relocation insights for women professionals

About

Dandelion helps women employees—especially those who are caregivers or in dual-income families—navigate relocation challenges through AI-powered support tools.

What We Offer

  • Simulated impact scenarios (childcare availability, partner career disruption)
  • Tailored relocation-readiness insights
  • Anonymous input of constraints and preferences

Why It Matters

Many women silently opt out of relocation due to invisible pressures. We make those voices visible—without forcing them to speak up.
